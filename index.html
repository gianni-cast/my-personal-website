Steelers 2024 Draft Class

The Steelers have overhauled their qb room in free agency. They moved on from their former 1st round draft selection, Kenny Pickett, and have aquired Russell Wilson along with Justin Fields. Additionally they signed former Falcons head coach (2021-23), Arthur Smith, to be their new offensive cordinator for upcoming 2024 NFL season. The hope is to replicate the success he had with the run game as OC of the Titans (2019-20). The Steelers had one of the best rushing attacks towards the end of 2023 season with the "Thunder" and "Lightning" tag team of Najee Harris and Jaylen Warren. The Steelers cleary prioritized their offensive line in this years draft as they added three new offensive lineman in addition to adding depth to other positions of need. Let's take a look at the 2024 Draft Class!

Round 01 Pick 20: Troy Fautanu

Round 02 Pick 51: Zach Frazier

Round 03 Pick 84: Roman Wilson

Round 03 Pick 97: Payton Wilson

Round 04 Pick 119: Mason McCormick

Round 06 Pick 178: Logan Lee

The Steelers have now had two excellent drafts in back to back years under new genral mananger, Omar Khan. Four of these players will most likely be day one starters in September and should contribute immediately. Russell Wilson and Justin Fields will have the time and protection to make big time plays and win a lot of Football games. The AFC north is currently the toughest division in football and the Steelers have a legitamate chance to once again be known as the, "Kings in the North".
